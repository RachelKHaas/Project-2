About Me

I’m Rachel Haas, a multidisciplinary designer currently working on my Master of Arts in Web Design and Online Communication at The University of Florida.

With a background in writing – graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in English from Florida State University in 2016 – I am venturing into digital media, combining my strong communication skills with a keen eye for design. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign programs, and am building upon a rudimentary understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript coding languages.

This website is the first that I have built from scratch, and future web building projects will be contained in this online portfolio. Stay tuned!
